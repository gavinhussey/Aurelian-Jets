Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

01

Information We Collect

When you request access to Aurelian Jets services, we collect:

  • Required: Full name, email address
  • Optional: City, state, phone number

We also automatically collect technical information including IP address, browser type, and device information when you visit our website.

02

How We Collect Information

Information is collected directly from you through:

  • Request Access form submissions
  • Email communications
  • Standard website technologies for functionality
03

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and respond to service access requests
  • Communicate about availability and service updates
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

You may opt out of non-essential communications at any time by following unsubscribe instructions or contacting us directly.

04

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • FAA-certified Part 135 air carriers for flight operations, when applicable
  • Service providers who assist with operations (e.g., email, data storage)
  • Legal authorities when required by law
05

Cookies

We use essential cookies required for basic website functionality. We may also use privacy-friendly analytics that do not track individuals or require cookie consent. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

06

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure. You provide information at your own risk.

07

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
08

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as reasonably necessary, generally no longer than 24 months unless required by law or ongoing service discussions. You may request deletion at any time.

09

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

11

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
