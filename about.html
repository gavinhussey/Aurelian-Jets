About Aurelian Jets

Redefining private aviation through reliability, transparency, and uncompromising service

01

Who We Are

Aurelian Jets is a private aviation brokerage built for clients who refuse to compromise. We connect discerning travelers with FAA-certified Part 135 operators, handling every detail from initial inquiry to wheels-up — and everything in between.

Our name draws from the Latin aureus — gold. It represents our standard: nothing less than excellence in every interaction, every flight, every outcome.

02

Our Mission

Private aviation should be seamless, not stressful. Too often, charter experiences are fragmented — unclear pricing, unreliable operators, and no accountability when things go wrong.

We exist to change that. Aurelian Jets provides a single point of coordination with complete transparency, vetted operators, and a commitment to getting you where you need to be — on time, every time.

03

What Sets Us Apart

We're not just another broker. We're your aviation partner.

  • Guaranteed Arrival: Our signature service builds redundancy into every critical trip. If your primary aircraft becomes unavailable, we activate backup solutions — because missing your moment is not an option.
  • Vetted Operators Only: Every carrier in our network is FAA-certified, independently verified, and held to the highest safety and service standards.
  • Transparent Pricing: No hidden fees. No surprise charges. You'll know exactly what you're paying for before you commit.
  • 24/7 Concierge Support: From booking to landing, our team is available around the clock. One call. One point of contact. Complete peace of mind.
  • Empty-Leg Intelligence: We monitor repositioning flights across our network, offering significant savings on one-way routes when schedules align.
04

Our Approach

We believe private aviation should adapt to you — not the other way around. That means:

  • Flexibility First: Plans change. We make adjustments easy, with clear policies and responsive service.
  • Discretion Always: Your travel is your business. We maintain strict confidentiality in every aspect of our service.
  • Relationships Over Transactions: We're building long-term partnerships, not processing bookings. Your preferences, your history, your priorities — we remember them all.
05

Who We Serve

Aurelian Jets serves individuals and organizations where time is the ultimate currency:

  • Executives and Entrepreneurs: Board meetings, investor pitches, site visits — when your schedule doesn't bend, neither do we.
  • Family Offices and High-Net-Worth Individuals: Multi-leg itineraries, last-minute changes, complete privacy. We handle complexity with ease.
  • Sports and Entertainment: Athletes, artists, and production teams with unforgiving schedules and zero margin for error.
  • Corporate Travel Departments: Streamlined booking, consolidated billing, and dedicated account management for organizations.
  • Medical and Emergency Transport: When time-sensitive travel is critical, we coordinate rapid response solutions with appropriate operators.
06

Our Network

We've built relationships with premier Part 135 operators across the United States and internationally. Our network includes:

  • Light jets for regional efficiency
  • Midsize jets for cross-country comfort
  • Super-midsize and heavy jets for transcontinental range
  • Ultra-long-range aircraft for international travel
  • Turboprops for short-field and regional access
  • VIP airliners for large groups and special events

Every operator is vetted for safety records, maintenance standards, crew qualifications, and service quality. We don't compromise — and neither should you.

07

Safety First

Safety is non-negotiable. Every operator in our network:

  • Holds current FAA Part 135 certification
  • Maintains comprehensive insurance coverage
  • Employs crews that meet or exceed regulatory minimums for experience and training
  • Operates aircraft under rigorous maintenance programs
  • Is subject to ongoing review and performance monitoring

We work exclusively with operators who share our commitment to safety, professionalism, and accountability.
